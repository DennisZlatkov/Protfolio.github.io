About Me

I'm a 2D animator / designer from Bulgaria who enjoys working with clients who appreciate quality.

A self-taught animator since 2018, I've worked on everything from small to large company projects.

I specialise in animation with Toon Boom Harmony as it's my favourite animation software, but I have excellent skills in Photoshop, Adobe Animate, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After effects and Blender 3D.
